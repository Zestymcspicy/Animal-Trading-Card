Nyan Cat

It fires a rainbow from its posterior enabling interplanetary flight.

  • Scientific Name: Poptatrticus Catticus
  • Average Length: 7.5 inches
  • Average Lifespan: Presumably Infinite
  • Habitat: Vacuum

Resembles an earth feline with the body of a frosted toaster pastry. Defends itself from massive space squid with a horribly obnoxious sound. Organs are all made out of artificial raspberry jam. Shouldn't be alive. Existence defies natural law.
